Goodbyes

Let's believe we will say hello again, because some goodbyes are harder to accept than others.

Every time I say goodbye, I let myself believe that when we say hello again, we will be just as good friends as we were then.

But some goodbyes will be forever. And some friendships will become fond memories.

But I think it's better that way, to let old friendships settle into happy memories rather than forced commitments.

Because when we meet again, we will both be different people. And with a certainty, we will be better off for the time apart - though we don't know it yet.

So to my dearest one, goodbye. Let's believe we will say hello again, because some goodbyes are harder to accept than others.
