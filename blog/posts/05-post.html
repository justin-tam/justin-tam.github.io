Beautiful

A Letter to my Child.

My dearest Child, You are my everything.
You are my heart, You are my soul.
You are the embodiment of my ambitions and my love.

Princes, Kings, and Emperors have bowed before you.
Men, women, boys, and girls have worshiped you.
And yet their praise falls short.

I've climbed the highest mountains.
And dived beneath the deepest oceans.
I've seen everything and met everyone.
And yet you have surpassed them all.

I was there when you came.
Screaming and crying into this world.
And I will see you when that world ends.
But for now, let me hold you.

You are beautiful.
No words, no pictures, no song, food, nor touch can show it.
You are beautiful.
And you always will be.

  • Justin Tam
