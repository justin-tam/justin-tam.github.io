Goodnight Sweet Moon

I wish we had more time, but the sun is rising. Sleep well, sweet moon. I'll see you when the sun lies.

To wish upon a star would not be enough,
To cast a coin into the fount would just be a bluff,
To pray to God almighty, now that's the right stuff,
God bless and keep you my love.

  • Justin Tam
