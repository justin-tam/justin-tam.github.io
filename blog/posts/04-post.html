Drink

To all my sweet sorrows! And to my quests left incomplete.

I sit here under the night sky.
How many times did I struggle for a better tomorrow?
How many days and nights did I starve so that we could survive?
Not a word. Not a sound. Nothing for the knight who stood alone against the darkness.
Aye. I'll take that drink. Another sip of that bittersweet ale.
Was it just a moment? A memory to be stored or forgotten?
No.
I'm drowning. Drowning in the darkness. Your darkness.
You said you didn't know... Until you did. Ha!
Just pass me another glass of that sweet melancholy.
- A Lost Knight
