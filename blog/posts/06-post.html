Wiser

Life has taught me so much about myself...

When I was a babe, I was needy.
I craved attention, attention I never received.

As a youth, I was bullied.
I sought acceptance, and found scorn instead.

As a young adult, I was popular.
I wanted love, and my peers admired and respected me.

As an adult, I was insecure.
I wanted stability, and stability was constantly out of reach.

Everything I have wanted, I have found.
But these things did not bring me contentment.
Not attention, acceptance, love, respect, stability.

I am good as I am. I always have been.
All the other things are important, but nothing and no one can change who I authentically am.
Complete, whole, and perfectly imperfect.

  • Justin Tam
